How Travel Agency Work

The customer contacts the travel agency to consult on travel goals, budget, preferences, and special needs. Its agents work with the customer to detail the details of the trip, including the destination, dates, accommodation, transportation, and desired activities. Travel agencies conduct in-depth research to find the best options according to customer needs. They work closely with suppliers such as airlines, hotels and tour operators to put together suitable travel packages. Once the customer approves the itinerary, the travel agency takes care of booking air, train, or other transportation tickets. They also make accommodation bookings as per the customer's preference.
